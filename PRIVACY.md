# Privacy Policy
AI Overview Purger does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

## Data Usage
This extension does not track users, collect analytics, or send any information to external servers.

## Permissions
- Storage: Used only to save the user's preference for enabling or disabling the extension.
- Host permissions (Google domains): Used only to modify the appearance of Google Search pages by removing AI overview elements.

## Third Parties
This extension does not use any third-party services, APIs, or remote code.

## Changes
This privacy policy may be updated as the extension evolves.
